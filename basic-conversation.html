Basic Korean Conversations

1. Introducing Oneself

These are the key phrases to introduce yourself in Korean:

  • 안녕하세요? (Annyeonghaseyo?) - Hello
  • 제 이름은 [Your Name]입니다. (Je ireumeun [Your Name]-imnida.) - My name is [Your Name].
  • 만나서 반갑습니다. (Mannaseo bangapseumnida.) - Nice to meet you.
  • 어디에서 오셨어요? (Eodieseo osyeosseoyo?) - Where are you from?
  • [Country]에서 왔어요. ([Country]-eseo wasseoyo.) - I am from [Country].

2. Asking for Directions

Learn how to ask and understand directions:

  • [Place] 어디에 있나요? ([Place] eodie itnayo?) - Where is [Place]?
  • 왼쪽으로 가세요. (Oenjjogeuro gaseyo.) - Go left.
  • 오른쪽으로 가세요. (Oreunjjogeuro gaseyo.) - Go right.
  • 직진하세요. (Jikjin haseyo.) - Go straight.
  • 가까운 지하철역이 어디예요? (Gakkaun jihacheolyeogi eodieyo?) - Where is the nearest subway station?

3. Ordering Food

These are common phrases for ordering food at a restaurant:

  • 메뉴 좀 주세요. (Menu jom juseyo.) - Please give me the menu.
  • [Dish Name] 주세요. ([Dish Name] juseyo.) - Please give me [Dish Name].
  • 물 좀 주세요. (Mul jom juseyo.) - Please give me some water.
  • 계산서 좀 주세요. (Gyesanseo jom juseyo.) - Please give me the bill.

4. Shopping and Bargaining

Useful sentences for shopping:

  • 이거 얼마예요? (Igeo eolmayeyo?) - How much is this?
  • 좀 더 싸게 해 주실 수 있나요? (Jom deo ssage hae jusil su innayo?) - Can you give me a discount?
  • 카드로 계산할 수 있나요? (Kadeuro gyesanhal su innayo?) - Can I pay by card?

5. Taking Public Transport

Phrases for navigating public transport:

  • 이 버스는 [Destination]에 가나요? (I beoseuneun [Destination]-e ganayo?) - Does this bus go to [Destination]?
  • 다음 역은 어디인가요? (Daum yeogeun eodiingayo?) - What is the next station?
  • 표 한 장 주세요. (Pyo han jang juseyo.) - One ticket, please.

6. Emergencies

Important phrases for emergencies:

  • 도와주세요! (Dowajuseyo!) - Help!
  • 경찰을 불러주세요. (Gyeongchareul bulleojuseyo.) - Please call the police.
  • 병원에 가야 해요. (Byeongwone gaya haeyo.) - I need to go to the hospital.
